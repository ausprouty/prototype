Summary

Read Hebrews 5:8-9.  We don’t have a lot of information about Jesus’ teenage life but we can assume he grew up in normal Jewish conditions.  He was the oldest brother of a large family (Mark 6:3).  He would have joined Joseph learning the family trade of being a builder.  The bible says that just like us “He learned obedience” (Hebrews 5:8).  In other words, he accepted correction and responded appropriately.  He was insulted by His brothers and was ignored by them.  After Joseph died, He would become the main provider for the family until His public ministry started (Mark 6:3).
